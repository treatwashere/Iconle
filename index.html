How to Play

  1. Look at the heavily blurred icon.
  2. Guess which brand or app it is.
  3. Each wrong guess reveals more of the icon.
  4. Daily Challenge gives everyone the same icon each day. Practice mode is unlimited random rounds.
  5. Turn on Hard Mode for fewer guesses and an extra challenge.

Example: typing "X", "Twitter", or "Elon" will all work for the X (formerly Twitter) brand.
